Data Collection

MD Step Wizard does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Our app is designed as a completely offline toolkit for molecular dynamics researchers. All calculations and tools work locally on your device without requiring any data transmission.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • User-generated content
  • Calculation results or parameters

Third-Party Services

MD Step Wizard uses SFSafariViewController to display external websites (Stanford profiles, LinkedIn, Google Scholar) when you tap profile links. These external websites have their own privacy policies:

  • Stanford University Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Local Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure within the app are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Scientific Purpose

MD Step Wizard is developed for educational and research purposes in the field of molecular dynamics simulations. The app contains calculators, parameter generators, and reference materials for GROMACS software.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from users of any age, including children under 13. The app is designed for researchers and students in computational biology.

Security

Since we don't collect any data, there are no data security concerns. All app functionality operates locally on your device.
